# AI-Assisted Assessment Boundaries

Date: 2026-07-18

## Current status

No live AI drafting integration was added in this phase.

The architecture now stores safe draft context for future AI-assisted workspace custom assessments through `workspace_custom_assessment_designs.ai_drafting_context`.

## Future permitted AI assistance

For workspace custom assessments, AI may later propose:

- sections;
- indicators;
- draft questions;
- response types;
- evidence prompts;
- descriptive report structure.

The user may provide:

- assessment purpose;
- target setting;
- country or jurisdiction;
- health scope;
- intended respondent;
- expected outputs;
- optional source documents or notes.

## Hard boundaries

AI-generated content remains:

- draft;
- workspace-owned;
- editable by the workspace;
- not approved by Vytte;
- not official methodology.

AI must not:

- approve official questions;
- publish official content;
- define official scoring;
- claim compliance with a standard;
- invent sources;
- turn customer content into official Vytte methodology.

## Official content

Official Vytte content can use AI only as a drafting or review support tool under human methodology governance.

Publication still requires Vytte Platform Admin authority and immutable versioning.
